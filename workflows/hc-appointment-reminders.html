01 — The Problem
Empty Slots Cost
Real Money

Every no-show is a lost consultation fee, a wasted doctor hour, and potentially a patient whose condition deteriorated between the cancelled and rescheduled appointment. The cost is clinical and financial.

🚫
30–40% No-Show Rate
Nigerian hospitals report no-show rates of 30–40% for outpatient appointments. Primary cause: patients forget. Secondary cause: unable to cancel easily. Both are completely solvable with automation.
💸
Revenue Lost
A 50-consultation-per-day clinic with 35% no-show rate loses ~17 appointments daily. At ₦5,000 per consultation: ₦85,000/day · ₦1.7M/month of pure revenue evaporation.
📞
Manual Reminder Calls
Receptionist calls every patient the day before. 40 calls, 30 minutes each to get through. Half go unanswered. Receptionist has time for little else. This is the wrong use of trained clinical admin staff.
🔄
No Waitlist Management
Patient cancels at 8am. Receptionist doesn't know who to call for the 10am slot. Appointment sits empty. A waitlist management system would have backfilled it automatically.
✅ n8n sends personalised WhatsApp/SMS reminders 48hrs and 2hrs before every appointment — in the patient's preferred language — with one-tap confirm, reschedule, or cancel.
