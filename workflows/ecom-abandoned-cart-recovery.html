01 — The Problem
75% of Your Shoppers
Leave Without Buying

You spent money to bring them to your store. They added items to their cart. Then they left. Without an automated recovery system, that revenue is gone. With one, it's the highest-ROI automation any e-commerce business can run.

🚪
The 75% Problem
Industry average cart abandonment rate is 70–75%. On ₦1M of "add to cart" activity, ₦750k walks out the door. Most Nigerian e-commerce stores have zero recovery sequence. This is recoverable revenue.
⏰
Timing Is Everything
Message sent 1 hour after abandonment converts 3× better than one sent 24 hours later. Manual recovery is impossible to execute consistently. By the time you notice, the window is closed.
🤖
Generic Messages Fail
"You left something in your cart!" sent to everyone, regardless of what they added, their purchase history, or their price sensitivity. Generic messages convert at 2–3%. Personalised ones convert at 15–25%.
📊
No Funnel Visibility
At what point do most people abandon? Which products have highest abandonment rates? Which recovery message performs best? Without data, optimisation is impossible.
✅ n8n fires a personalised 3-touch recovery sequence: 1hr, 24hr, and 72hr. GPT-4o writes unique messages based on abandoned items, customer history, and price sensitivity. Sequence stops automatically on purchase.
02 — The Blueprint
n8n Cart Recovery
Sequence

Shopper abandons cart → n8n detects in real time → 3-message personalised sequence fires → purchase detected → sequence stops. Revenue recovered automatically at 15–25% rate.

n8n canvas — abandoned cart recovery pipeline
Detect
🛒
Cart
Abandon
Website/app fires webhook when cart is abandoned (session ends without purchase, or user closes tab after 10 mins of inactivity on checkout page). n8n captures cart contents, value, and customer identity.
→
Profile
🧠
GPT-4o
Profile
GPT-4o builds recovery profile: first-time or returning customer? High-value or budget items? Previous purchase history? Discount-sensitive? Sets message tone and discount strategy accordingly.
💡
Discount
Strategy
High-value customer: no discount needed — urgency messaging. Price-sensitive: 10% off at 24hr message. Budget items: free shipping offer. AI sets strategy to maximise margin while driving conversion.
→
Recover
📱
T+1hr
WhatsApp
Message 1 (1hr): Warm reminder. "Hi [Name], you left something behind! Your [item] is waiting." + cart image + direct checkout link. No pressure, no discount.
📧
T+24hr
Email
Message 2 (24hr): Social proof + urgency. "Other shoppers are looking at this." + review quotes + "Only 3 left in stock" if true. Discount offer if profile suggests price-sensitive.
→
Final
🔥
T+72hr
Last Chance
Message 3 (72hr): Final urgency. "Your cart expires in 24 hours." Best offer (free delivery + discount if not yet converted). After this — sequence ends, cart cleared.
✅
Purchase
Stop
Any purchase at any point in sequence: all further messages cancelled immediately. No awkward "you left this in your cart" message after they've bought.
