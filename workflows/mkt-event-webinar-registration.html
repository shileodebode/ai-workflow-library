01 — The Problem
You Fill the Room.
Then Lose the Lead.

Events and webinars are among the highest-intent marketing touchpoints available. Yet most companies handle registration manually and lose 40–60% of attendees to no-shows — then fail to follow up the rest.

📧
Confirmation Chaos
Manual confirmation emails, calendar invites sent one by one, Zoom links copy-pasted into individual replies. Intern spends 3 hours confirming 80 registrants. Error-prone and unscalable.
👻
No-Show Rate
Average webinar no-show rate: 40–60%. The biggest driver is forgetting. A single well-timed personalised reminder can increase attendance by 35%. Most companies send zero or one generic reminder.
🏃
Post-Event Ghost
Webinar ends. Recording sent 3 days later. No personalised follow-up, no segmentation between attendees and no-shows, no conversion sequence. The highest-intent moment in your funnel left completely cold.
📊
No Attendance Data
Who attended? For how long? Which questions did they ask? Without this data, you can't prioritise follow-up, personalise outreach, or demonstrate event ROI to leadership.
✅ n8n automates every stage: instant confirmation, personalised reminders, live attendance tracking, and targeted post-event sequences — split by attendee vs no-show.
