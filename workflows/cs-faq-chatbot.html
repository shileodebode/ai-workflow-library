01 — The Problem
The Same 10 Questions.
1,000 Times.

The majority of support tickets are variations of the same questions. Your agents answer them manually, all day, every day — when a bot could handle them in 3 seconds.

🔁
Repetitive Questions
"How do I reset my password?" "What's your refund policy?" "Where's my order?" The top 10 question types cover 65–70% of all support volume. Agents answer them manually thousands of times a week.
🌙
No Out-of-Hours Support
Customers in different time zones or late-night shoppers hit a wall. No chat, no answer — just a form and a 24-hour wait. Meanwhile, competitors have 24/7 chat live.
📞
Agent Burnout
Talented support agents spend 60%+ of their time on questions any FAQ page could answer. Low engagement, high turnover, and wasted expertise on problems that shouldn't need a human.
🌍
Language Barriers
Global customer base, English-only support. Customers struggle to explain issues clearly. Agents struggle to understand them. CSAT drops. GPT-4o resolves this entirely.
✅ n8n + GPT-4o builds a chatbot trained on your knowledge base that resolves 70% of questions instantly — and hands off complex ones to the right human agent seamlessly.

n8n FAQ Chatbot
Architecture

Every conversation handled intelligently. Simple questions resolved instantly. Complex ones escalated with full context — no dead ends, no lost conversations.

n8n canvas — FAQ chatbot pipeline
Receive
🌐
Website

📱
WhatsApp
Bot
WhatsApp Business API integration. Customer messages trigger n8n workflow. Replies sent from your business number.
→
Understand
🧠
GPT-4o
Intent
GPT-4o classifies intent: FAQ/Account/Billing/Technical/Complaint/Human-needed. Extracts key entities (order number, email, product).
📚
KB
Search
Vector search across your knowledge base (Notion, Confluence, Help articles). Returns most relevant chunks for GPT-4o to synthesise.
→
Decide
🔀
Confidence
Router
If KB match confidence >85%: auto-answer. 60–85%: answer with "did this help?" check. <60% or "human" intent: escalate to live agent.
→
Respond
💬
Auto
Answer
GPT-4o composes a natural, brand-voice response using KB content. Not a canned reply — a contextual answer in your company's tone.
👤
Human
Handoff
On escalation: full conversation history sent to agent in Zendesk. Agent sees everything — no "please repeat your issue" moments.
→
Learn
⭐
CSAT
Collect
After resolution: one-click satisfaction rating. Negative ratings logged for KB improvement review. Positive ratings help train confidence thresholds.
📊
Analytics
Log
Every conversation logged: intent, resolution type (auto/human), confidence score, CSAT. Dashboard shows top topics, escalation rate, improvement opportunities.
