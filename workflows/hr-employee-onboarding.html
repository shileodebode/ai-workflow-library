01 — The Challenge
Onboarding is Broken
in Most Companies

Poor onboarding costs companies in retention, productivity, and employee satisfaction — and most of it is caused by manual, forgettable processes.

📋
Forgotten Tasks
HR forgets to request laptop setup, create email accounts, or assign desk. New hire arrives to no computer, no access, and a very bad first impression.
📄
Document Chaos
Employment contracts, tax forms, NDAs sent via random emails. No tracking of what's been signed, what's missing, what's expired.
👤
No Human Welcome
New hires feel invisible. No structured introduction to the team, culture, or tools. 20% of new hires quit within 45 days — onboarding is cited as primary reason.
🔑
IT Access Delays
IT tickets raised manually — often forgotten. New hires spend their first week chasing email passwords and Slack invites instead of contributing.
✅ n8n orchestrates every onboarding step — from offer letter to 90-day check-in. Nothing falls through the cracks, ever.
