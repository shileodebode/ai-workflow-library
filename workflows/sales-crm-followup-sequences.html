What Happens on Reply

  • 1
    Gmail reply detected → n8n webhook fires immediately
  • 2
    GPT-4o classifies reply: Positive / Negative / Question / Unsubscribe
  • 3
    Sequence paused — no more automated emails until rep acts
  • 4
    CRM deal stage updated based on reply classification
  • 5
    Slack alert sent to rep with reply content + AI summary + suggested next action
