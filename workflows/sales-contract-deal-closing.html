01 — The Problem
Deals Die Between
"Yes" & Signed

Verbal commitment isn't revenue. The gap between a prospect saying yes and a contract being signed is where deals leak — through delays, errors, and lost momentum.

⏳
Contract Delay
Legal team backlog means contracts take 3–5 days to generate. Prospect enthusiasm cools. Competitor gets back in the conversation. Deal momentum lost.
📝
Wrong Terms
Rep fills out template manually — wrong payment terms, wrong entity name, wrong start date. Legal catches it. Contract reissued. Another 2 days gone.
🔄
No Countersign Flow
Contract sent, client signs, nobody notices. No internal countersignature process. Deal sits in legal limbo — technically unsigned by your own company.
📁
Scattered Records
Signed contracts in email attachments, shared drives, Dropbox folders. No central record. Legal spends days hunting for contracts during audits or disputes.
✅ n8n generates pre-populated, legally accurate contracts in minutes, routes for signature automatically, and files everything centrally on sign.
