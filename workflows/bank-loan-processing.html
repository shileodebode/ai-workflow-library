01 — The Problem
Loan Decisions That
Take Two Weeks

Manual loan processing is the growth ceiling for every lending institution. Backlogs, inconsistent decisions, and slow disbursements drive creditworthy borrowers to competitors who answer in hours.

📚
Manual Underwriting Backlog
Each application takes 2–3 hours of analyst time: verifying income, calculating DTI ratio, checking credit bureau, assessing collateral. 50 applications per week = two full-time analysts doing nothing else.
⏰
Slow Decisions Lose Customers
Applicant applies for ₦500k business loan. Decision promised in 5 business days. Competitor fintech approves in 2 hours. Customer takes the faster offer. Your institution loses the loan and the customer relationship.
🎭
Inconsistent Decisions
Analyst A approves a 35% DTI ratio on a Friday. Analyst B declines the same profile on Monday. Subjective underwriting creates inconsistency, regulatory exposure, and potential discrimination risk.
📊
No Real-time Portfolio View
NPL rate unknown until month-end. Sector concentration risk invisible. Which loan officer has the highest default rate in their book? Manual tracking makes these questions unanswerable in real time.
✅ n8n automates the entire lending pipeline: application ingestion, bureau pull, credit scoring, offer generation, agreement execution, disbursement, and repayment tracking.
02 — The Blueprint
n8n Loan Processing
Pipeline

Application received → scored → decisioned → offered → signed → disbursed → repayment monitored. Every step automated. Humans involved only for borderline cases.

n8n canvas — loan processing pipeline
Apply
📱
Digital
Application
Borrower completes mobile loan application: amount, purpose, repayment period, income details. Uploads: pay slips, bank statements, employer letter. All stored and structured by n8n.
→
Assess
🧠
GPT-4o
Income Parse
GPT-4o reads bank statements: calculates average monthly income, identifies salary credits, flags irregular income. Calculates DTI (debt-to-income) ratio automatically.
📊
Bureau
Pull
Credit bureau (CRC/FirstCentral) queried via API. Score, existing loans, payment history, and defaults all pulled automatically. Adds to credit decision model.
→
Score
🎯
Credit
Score
Proprietary scoring model: weights bureau score, DTI, income stability, tenure, purpose of loan, and collateral if any. Score 0–1000. Approve / Review / Decline thresholds set by risk team.
→
Decide
✅
Auto
Approve
Score ≥680: auto-approved. Offer letter generated: loan amount, interest rate, tenure, repayment schedule. Sent to borrower via WhatsApp for e-signature acceptance.
👤
Officer
Review
Score 550–679: routed to loan officer with full analysis pre-populated. Officer reviews and decides. No manual data gathering — everything ready when they open the case.
→
Disburse
💰
Auto
Disburse
E-signature received → loan agreement executed → disbursement initiated via Paystack/bank transfer within 2 hours. Borrower receives SMS confirmation with repayment schedule.
📊
Repayment
Monitor
Daily repayment monitoring. 3 days before due: WhatsApp reminder. Missed payment: escalation to collections workflow. Portfolio NPL tracked in real time dashboard.
