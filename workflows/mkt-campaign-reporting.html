01 — The Problem
Reporting That Takes
All Friday

Marketing teams spend 4–6 hours every week pulling data from different dashboards, copying numbers into slides, and writing summaries. None of that time creates value.

🔄
Manual Data Pulling
Google Analytics, Meta Ads, LinkedIn, email platform, CRM — all separate dashboards. Copy numbers into Excel, cross-reference, calculate rates. 3 hours minimum, every week, error-prone.
📊
Stale Insights
Weekly report shows last week's data presented on Friday. Leadership makes decisions on 5-day-old information. Fast-moving campaigns keep spending on what was already underperforming.
🎭
Numbers, Not Insights
Reports full of numbers with no interpretation. "CTR was 2.4%" — so what? Is that good? What should change? Marketers present data; nobody knows what to do with it.
👻
Missed Anomalies
CPA spiked 40% on Tuesday. Nobody noticed until Friday's report. £3,000 wasted on a broken campaign segment. Real-time anomaly detection would have caught it in 2 hours.
✅ n8n pulls data from every channel automatically, GPT-4o writes the analysis and recommendations, and a full report lands in inboxes every Monday at 8am — no human effort required.
