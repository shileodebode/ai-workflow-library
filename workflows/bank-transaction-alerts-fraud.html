01 — The Problem
Fraud Discovered
After the Damage

Most financial institutions detect fraud after it has occurred — in end-of-day batch runs, complaint calls, or monthly reconciliation. By then, the money is gone and customer trust is broken.

🕐
Delayed Detection
Fraud detected in batch overnight run — 6 to 12 hours after transaction. ₦2M has already been moved through three accounts and withdrawn. Real-time monitoring would have caught it in the first transaction.
📵
Customer Unaware
Customer's card used in Port Harcourt while they're in Abuja. No alert. Customer finds out at month-end when they see the statement. By then, ₦180k is gone and the chargeback window may be closed.
🎭
False Positive Flood
Rule-based fraud systems block legitimate large transfers, triggering customer calls and frustration. AI-based systems learn customer behaviour patterns and dramatically reduce false positives while catching more real fraud.
📊
No Fraud Intelligence
Which fraud vectors are increasing? Which customer segments are most vulnerable? Without real-time analytics, fraud strategy is reactive and the same schemes repeat quarter after quarter.
✅ n8n monitors every transaction in real time, sends instant alerts to customers, runs AI fraud scoring on each transaction, auto-blocks high-risk activity, and routes cases to investigators instantly.
