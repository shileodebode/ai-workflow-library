01 — The Problem
Your Lead Magnets
Are Leaking

You spent time creating the perfect checklist, guide, or webinar. Hundreds of people download it. Then most of them disappear — because nothing follows up fast enough or well enough.

📥
Download & Disappear
Someone downloads your lead magnet — the highest intent signal they'll ever give before buying. You send one generic welcome email, then nothing. The moment of peak interest passes unused.
🎭
Generic Sequences
Same 5-email drip to every lead regardless of what they downloaded, how engaged they are, or what stage they're at. Cold leads and hot leads get identical treatment.
👻
Hot Lead Blindspot
Someone opens every email, clicks every link, visits your pricing page twice. Nobody knows. Sales team isn't told. The lead goes cold because no one reached out at the right moment.
🚿
Leaky Funnel
No re-engagement for leads who stop opening. No branching for leads who engage heavily. One-size-fits-all sequences leak revenue at every stage of the funnel.
✅ n8n detects every download, triggers a personalised sequence, scores engagement in real time, and alerts sales the moment a lead hits the hot threshold.
