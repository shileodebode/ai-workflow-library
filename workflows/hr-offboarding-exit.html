01 — The Risk
Offboarding Done Wrong
Is a Liability

Most companies have no formal offboarding process. The result: security breaches, unhappy exits, and expensive compliance failures.

🔓
Security Breach Risk
Ex-employees with active Google, Slack, or CRM access. 63% of data breaches involve former employees whose accounts were never deactivated.
📋
Knowledge Loss
Critical process knowledge walks out the door. No handover document, no knowledge base article, no team brief. Projects stall for weeks.
💼
Equipment Not Returned
Laptops, phones, and access cards disappear. No tracking, no reminder system, no consequence. Thousands of pounds in unrecovered assets.
📊
No Exit Intelligence
Why are people leaving? Nobody knows because exit surveys are manual, inconsistently done, and the data is never aggregated. AI changes this.
✅ n8n triggers a complete offboarding protocol the moment resignation is logged — access revoked, surveys sent, handover assigned. All in under one hour.
