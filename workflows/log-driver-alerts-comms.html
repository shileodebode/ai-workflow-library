01 — The Problem
Driver Comms That
Fail Everyone

The gap between what the operations team knows and what drivers know is where accidents happen, deliveries are missed, and cargo gets lost. Manual radio and phone calls don't scale.

📻
Radio & Phone Chaos
Operations calls every driver individually to brief them on routes, check their status, or relay customer changes. 20 drivers × 3 calls each = 60 calls a day. Half go unanswered. Nothing is documented.
👻
Silent Drivers
Driver broke down 3 hours ago. Didn't call in. Operations finds out when the customer calls asking where their goods are. By then, a replacement driver could have been dispatched 2 hours ago.
🗺️
No Route Update System
Road closed on A1 Expressway. Operations knows. Driver doesn't. Spends 45 minutes in a jam before calling in. A proactive WhatsApp update would have had him on the alternative route before he reached the junction.
📋
Undocumented Incidents
Minor accident. Driver notes it verbally on return. Nothing documented. Insurance claim later. Operations has no contemporaneous record of the incident. Claim disputed. Revenue lost.
✅ n8n sends every driver their briefing at dispatch, monitors check-ins automatically, pushes route alerts in real time, and captures incident reports via WhatsApp — fully documented.
