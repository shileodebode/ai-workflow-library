01 — The Problem
Feedback You Never
Actually Receive

Most businesses think they understand their customer experience. Most don't — because they ask at the wrong time, analyse nothing, and act on even less.

⏰
Wrong Timing
Monthly bulk surveys. Quarterly NPS blasts. By the time you ask, the customer has forgotten the experience — or it's been too long and they've churned. Timing is everything.
📊
Unread Reports
Survey responses go into a spreadsheet or a dashboard nobody opens. The feedback exists but is never acted on. The same issues recur quarter after quarter.
😤
Negative Feedback Ignored
A customer rates you 1 star and writes a scathing comment. Nobody sees it for 3 weeks. No follow-up, no rescue — just a lost customer who feels unheard.
🔇
Low Response Rates
Generic survey links sent to everyone. 6% open rate. 1.5% completion. The feedback you do get is unrepresentative — mostly extreme opinions, nothing useful in the middle.
✅ n8n sends surveys automatically at resolution, analyses every response with GPT-4o, escalates negative feedback instantly, and builds a live CSAT dashboard.
02 — The Blueprint
n8n Survey Automation
Workflow

Ticket resolved → survey sent → response analysed → insight acted on. The whole loop automated, every time, for every customer.

n8n canvas — CSAT survey pipeline
Trigger
✅
Ticket
Resolved
Zendesk/Freshdesk webhook fires when ticket status changes to Resolved. Triggers survey pipeline for all ticket types.
⏰
Timed
Wait
n8n waits 30 minutes after resolution before sending survey. Gives customer time to confirm the solution works — increases honest positive responses.
→
Personalise
🧠
GPT-4o
Tailor
GPT-4o personalises the survey: uses customer name, references the specific issue resolved, and adjusts tone based on ticket sentiment history.
🎯
Channel
Select
Survey sent via the same channel the ticket was raised: Email ticket → email survey. Chat ticket → in-chat rating. WhatsApp ticket → WhatsApp survey.
→
Collect
⭐
Rating +
Comment
One-click star rating (1–5) + optional text comment. Simple enough for 90%+ completion rate. Low friction = high response rate.
→
Analyse
🧠
GPT-4o
Analyse
GPT-4o categorises response: Positive/Neutral/Negative. Extracts themes: wait time, agent quality, resolution effectiveness. Flags agent coaching opportunities.
🚨
Alert
Router
1-2 star: CS manager notified immediately + save-the-customer email drafted. 3 star: logged for review. 4-5 star: logged + agent recognition message sent.
→
Act
📊
CSAT
Dashboard
All responses feed live dashboard: overall CSAT score, trend over time, by agent, by ticket type. Weekly digest sent to CS manager every Monday.
🔁
Training
Loop
Recurring negative themes automatically surfaced to CS manager monthly. Agent coaching triggers for low-rated interactions. Knowledge base gaps flagged.
