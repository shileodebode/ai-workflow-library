01 — The Problem
Hiring is Broken
Without Automation

Traditional recruitment drowns HR teams in paperwork, email, and repetitive screening — while great candidates get ignored for days.

📥
CV Overload
A single role attracts 200+ applications. HR manually opens each CV, reads it, and decides — 3-5 mins per CV = 10+ hrs of screening per role.
⏳
Slow Response Times
Top candidates accept competitor offers while waiting 2–3 weeks for acknowledgement. Speed of response is now a hiring differentiator.
🎯
Inconsistent Scoring
Different HR reviewers apply different standards. Monday's tired reviewer scores differently than Friday's. AI applies the same rubric every time.
📊
No Pipeline Visibility
Applications scattered across email inboxes. No central view of where each candidate stands. Pipeline data lives in someone's head.
✅ Our n8n + GPT-4o workflow solves all four — screening 200 CVs in the time it used to take to read 3.
02 — The Blueprint
n8n Recruitment
Workflow

Every application enters the pipeline at Stage 1. AI handles screening, scoring, and communication. HR only sees the shortlist.

n8n canvas — recruitment pipeline
Capture
📧
Email
Applications
Gmail trigger watches for emails with subject containing "Application for" or attachments named CV/Resume.
🌐
Job Board
Webhook
Webhooks from LinkedIn, Indeed, company careers page — all funnelled into one n8n entry point.
→
Extract
📄
PDF/Doc
Parser
n8n extracts text from CV attachments (PDF/DOCX). Parses name, email, phone, experience, skills, education into structured JSON.
→
AI Screen
🧠
GPT-4o
Scorer
GPT-4o compares CV against job description. Returns score 0–100, matched skills, gaps, and recommendation: SHORTLIST / REJECT / HOLD.
🔀
IF Router
(Score)
Routes: Score ≥70 → Shortlist. Score 50–69 → Hold. Score <50 → Reject. Each path triggers different actions.
→
Act
📊

Log
All candidates logged to Google Sheets CRM: name, score, status, role, date, AI notes. Real-time pipeline for HR team.
📅
Calendly
Interview
Shortlisted candidates receive personalised Calendly link for interview scheduling — no HR back-and-forth needed.
📧
Auto
Reject
Rejected candidates receive a warm, professional decline email within minutes of submission — not weeks of silence.
→
Notify
💬
Slack
Shortlist

📋
HR
Dashboard
Google Sheets auto-generates a live dashboard showing pipeline health: total applicants, shortlisted %, time-to-shortlist average.
