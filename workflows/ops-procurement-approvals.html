01 — The Problem
Procurement That
Nobody Controls

Without a proper procurement system, spending is reactive, inconsistent, and invisible until it shows up on a credit card statement. Approval processes that live in email are no processes at all.

🎭
Shadow Purchasing
Staff buy what they need and submit expenses later. No pre-approval, no budget check, no vendor validation. Operations discovers it spent ₦2M on software nobody knew was being purchased.
📧
Approval by Email Chain
Purchase request emailed to manager. Manager cc's finance. Finance replies asking for justification. Another email. Three days later, someone approves verbally on a call. No audit trail, no consistency.
💸
No Budget Visibility
Department heads approve purchases without knowing their remaining budget. Finance only finds out about overruns at month-end. By then, the money is already spent.
📄
Manual PO Creation
After approval, someone manually creates a purchase order in the accounting system. Copy-paste errors, wrong codes, missing fields. Takes 30 minutes per PO. Completely unnecessary with automation.
✅ n8n enforces procurement policy on every request, routes through the right approval chain, checks budget in real time, and auto-generates the PO on approval — with a full audit trail.
