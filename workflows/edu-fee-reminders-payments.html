01 — The Problem
Fee Collection That
Hurts Relationships

Chasing school fees is the most uncomfortable task in education administration. It strains relationships with families, consumes staff time, and creates cash flow uncertainty. Automation removes the human awkwardness entirely.

😬
Awkward Chasing
Bursar calls parents individually to remind them of outstanding fees. Each call is uncomfortable. Parents feel embarrassed. Staff feel guilty. The automation handles this diplomatically — no human awkwardness needed.
📊
No Real-Time Collection View
How much has been collected this term? Which students are overdue? What's the total outstanding? The bursar has no answer without manually checking each record. Live dashboards change this immediately.
🧾
Manual Receipt Issuance
Parent pays. Bursar manually records payment. Writes or prints receipt. Files it. Emails it. 15 minutes per payment × 200 families = 50 hours per term of pure administrative work.
💸
Instalment Tracking Nightmare
30 families on payment plans. Each on different schedules. Each requiring separate tracking. One Excel spreadsheet trying to hold it all together. Payments missed, discrepancies discovered at term-end.
✅ n8n sends personalised fee reminders with payment links, tracks instalments automatically, issues receipts instantly on payment, and escalates persistent non-payers to management — all without human involvement.
