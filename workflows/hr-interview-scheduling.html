01 — THE PROBLEM
SCHEDULING HELL

Interview coordination is one of the most time-consuming, error-prone tasks in recruitment. And it's 100% automatable.

📧
Email Tennis
Average 6–8 emails to schedule a single interview. "Are you free Tuesday?" "I can do Wednesday." "Actually Tuesday works..." — all automatable.
🗓️
Calendar Conflicts
HR books a slot, interviewer isn't available, candidate is told to reschedule. No real-time calendar availability check = constant back-tracking.
🔔
No-shows & Forgetting
Candidates forget their interview. No reminder system. 20–30% no-show rate on manually scheduled interviews — fixed instantly with automated reminders.
📋
Pre-Interview Prep Gap
Candidates arrive unprepared because no one sent them role details, interviewer name, or what to expect. AI can send a full prep pack automatically.
✅ n8n + Calendly + GPT-4o eliminates all four — candidates book, get reminded, and arrive prepared. HR does nothing.
