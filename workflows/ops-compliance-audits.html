01 — The Problem
Compliance Managed
in Spreadsheets

Most organisations manage compliance reactively — chasing signatures before audits, discovering expired certificates during due diligence, and scrambling to reconstruct audit trails from email chains.

📅
Expired Certificates
NAFDAC licence, PENCOM registration, ISO certificate, fire safety certificate — all with different expiry dates. Manually tracked in a spreadsheet someone last updated 8 months ago. One expired = audit failure.
📝
Policy Acknowledgements
AML policy updated. All 120 staff need to read and sign. Compliance manager emails everyone individually, tracks responses in an Excel sheet, and chases non-responders manually. Takes weeks.
🔍
No Audit Trail
Auditor asks: "Who approved this transaction on March 8th?" Finance searches emails for 3 hours. Without automated audit logging, every compliance question requires an investigation.
🚨
Reactive Discovery
Compliance gaps discovered during external audits — not by your own monitoring. By then, the cost is fines, remediation plans, and reputational damage. Prevention is always cheaper than cure.
✅ n8n monitors every compliance deadline, auto-collects policy signatures, maintains a complete audit log, and alerts management 30 days before any item expires or lapses.
