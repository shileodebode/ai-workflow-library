01 — The Problem
Vendor Onboarding
Takes Forever

Every new vendor is a potential compliance risk. But the manual process of collecting documents, verifying registrations, and getting approvals is so slow that operations are delayed waiting for vendors to be cleared.

📦
Document Black Hole
Procurement emails vendors requesting: CAC certificate, TIN, bank details, insurance certificate, and director IDs. Vendors respond partially. Procurement chases the rest. Average time to full pack: 3 weeks.
🔍
No Compliance Verification
CAC registration number typed by vendor — nobody verifies it with the registry. Bank account details accepted on trust. TIN numbers never checked against FIRS database. Significant fraud and error risk.
🔄
Manual Approval Chain
Completed vendor pack sent to Finance for review, then Legal, then Procurement Director. Each sits in inboxes. Multi-week approval chain before first purchase order can be raised.
📊
No Vendor Master
Vendor details scattered across emails, Dropbox folders, and one person's inbox. Finance can't find payment details. Procurement re-onboards the same vendor twice. No single source of truth.
✅ n8n automates document collection, CAC verification, bank validation, compliance checks, and approval routing — vendor onboarded in 48 hours with a complete digital record.
