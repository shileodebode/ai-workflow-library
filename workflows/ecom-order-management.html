01 — The Problem
Orders Across
5 Channels

Modern Nigerian e-commerce runs across website, Instagram, WhatsApp, Jumia, and Konga simultaneously. Without automation, orders get missed, confirmations are late, and fulfilment is chaos.

📱
Channel Chaos
Website order in one system. WhatsApp order in DMs. Jumia order in their dashboard. Konga in theirs. Instagram in comments. No unified view. Orders processed in whatever order someone happens to check each platform.
⏰
Slow Confirmations
Customer orders at 8pm. Business owner sees it at 10am next day. Sends confirmation at 11am. Customer has already reordered from a competitor who confirmed in 5 minutes. Speed of confirmation = trust signal.
📦
Fulfilment Errors
Order details manually passed to the warehouse team via WhatsApp. Wrong item sent. Wrong address. No barcode scan, no packing checklist, no double-check. Returns and refunds cost 3× the original transaction.
📊
No Order Analytics
Which products are selling fastest? Which channel has highest AOV? Which courier has best delivery rate? Without unified order data, inventory decisions and marketing spend are pure guesswork.
✅ n8n unifies orders from all channels into one pipeline, sends instant confirmation, generates packing slips, coordinates dispatch, and keeps customers updated from click to delivery.
