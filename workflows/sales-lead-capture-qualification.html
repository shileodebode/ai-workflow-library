01 — The Problem
Your Leads Are
Slipping Away

Companies spend thousands generating leads, then lose them to slow follow-up, poor qualification, and zero routing logic.

⏱️
The 5-Minute Rule
Studies show leads contacted within 5 minutes are 9x more likely to convert. Most companies respond in 24–48 hours. That gap costs deals.
🎯
No Qualification System
Sales reps spend 60% of their time chasing unqualified leads. No scoring system means senior reps waste hours on tyre-kickers.

Wrong Rep Assignment
Leads routed by round-robin or whoever picks up first. Enterprise leads go to junior reps. SMB leads go to enterprise specialists. Conversion tanks.

Multi-Channel Chaos
Leads coming from website, LinkedIn, referrals, events, cold email — tracked in 5 different places. No unified view, no priority order.
✅ n8n unifies all lead sources, scores every lead with GPT-4o, and routes to the right rep in under 90 seconds — automatically.

n8n Lead Pipeline
Architecture

n8n canvas — lead capture pipeline
Capture

Website
Form
Webhook fires on any website form submission — contact, demo request, pricing inquiry. All routed to same n8n entry point.
💼
LinkedIn
Leads
LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms API sends leads directly to n8n. Includes campaign source, ad creative, and LinkedIn profile data.
📧
Email
Inbound
Gmail trigger detects sales inquiry emails. GPT-4o extracts contact details and intent from free-text email body.
→
Enrich
🔍
Clearbit
Enrich
Clearbit API enriches lead with: company size, industry, revenue, tech stack, LinkedIn URL. Turns an email into a full profile.
🏢
Company
Lookup
Apollo.io or Hunter API adds company data: employee count, funding round, decision-maker contacts, similar companies.
→
AI Score
🧠
GPT-4o
BANT Score
GPT-4o scores lead 0-100 on BANT: Budget (company size/funding), Authority (seniority), Need (inquiry type), Timeline (urgency signals). Returns structured JSON.
🔀
Hot/Warm/
Cold Router
Score ≥75: HOT → senior rep + immediate call. Score 45-74: WARM → automated nurture + rep follow-up. Score <45: COLD → nurture sequence only.
→
Route
👤
Rep
Assignment
Routes based on: deal size → senior/junior rep, industry → specialist rep, geography → regional rep. No more round-robin guessing.
📊
CRM
Create
HubSpot / Salesforce contact and deal created automatically with all enriched data, score, source, and assignment pre-filled.
→
Alert
💬
Slack
Alert
Rep gets Slack alert with lead summary: name, company, score, inquiry, enrichment data. One-click to open in CRM or call.
📱
SMS
Hot Leads
HOT leads (score ≥75) also trigger an SMS to the assigned rep's phone. 5-minute response window starts now.
