01 — The Problem
Social Media Eats
Time It Shouldn't

A consistent, multi-platform social presence is essential for brand awareness and inbound leads. But maintaining it manually is a full-time job your marketing team doesn't have time for.

⏰
Manual Adaptation
The same message needs to sound completely different on LinkedIn vs Instagram vs Twitter. Writing four versions of every post manually takes 2–3 hours per piece of content.
📉
Inconsistent Posting
Busy weeks mean social goes silent. Algorithm punishes inconsistency. Reach drops. Follower growth stalls. A posting gap of 2 weeks can take months of recovery to fix.
📊
No Optimal Timing
Posting when it's convenient, not when your audience is active. LinkedIn posts at 5pm Friday. Instagram stories at 11am Monday. Sub-optimal timing kills engagement rates.
📁
No Content Calendar
No visibility of what's planned, what's been posted, or what's performing. Campaign consistency breaks down. Brand voice inconsistent across platforms and team members.
✅ n8n + GPT-4o turns one content brief into four platform-native posts, schedules them at optimal times, and publishes automatically — without a single manual step.
