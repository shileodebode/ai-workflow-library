01 — The Problem
Month-End Reporting
Costs a Week

Financial reporting is critical for governance and decision-making. But most finance teams spend 40+ hours manually compiling reports that could be automated — generating stale, late data in the process.

⏳
40-Hour Month-End
Pulling from Xero, reconciling entries, building P&L in Excel, formatting balance sheet, writing commentary, creating PDF. Finance teams spend the first week of every month on last month's numbers.
📉
Stale Decisions
Board receives March financials on April 18th. Decisions made on 18-day-old data. A cash flow problem visible on March 5th is actioned on April 20th. The lag is a strategic liability.
📄
Numbers Without Narrative
Spreadsheets of numbers with no interpretation. "Revenue was ₦12.4M" — compared to what? Why? What changed? Finance presents data; leadership doesn't know what to do with it.
🔒
Single Point of Failure
One finance manager knows how to build the report. They're sick for a week in March. The board meeting is delayed. Automation removes the dependency on any single person.
✅ n8n pulls financial data automatically on the 1st, GPT-4o writes the executive commentary, and a board-ready PDF is delivered by the 5th — every single month, no exceptions.
