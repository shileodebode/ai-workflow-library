01 — The Problem
Where Is My
Shipment?

The most common customer service complaint for logistics companies is lack of visibility. Customers don't know where their goods are, when they'll arrive, or why there's a delay. This is entirely avoidable.

📞
The "Where Is It?" Call
Customer service teams field hundreds of "tracking" calls daily. Each one takes 5–10 minutes and requires logging into multiple systems to give an answer that an automated WhatsApp update should have already provided.
🕐
Delay Discovery Too Late
Truck broke down at Ore, Ondo State. Driver doesn't report immediately. Operations only finds out when the delivery window passes. Customer has been waiting all day for goods that are 200km away.
📋
Manual Waybill Updates
Waybills updated manually at each hub. Information entered hours after the event. Tracking data always 4–6 hours behind reality. Live tracking impossible when the source data is stale.
📊
No Performance Data
On-time delivery rate unknown. Average transit time per route unknown. Which routes are consistently delayed — unknown. Operational improvement impossible without the data to drive it.
✅ n8n captures location updates from drivers' phones and hub check-ins, pushes automatic WhatsApp updates to customers, and detects delays before the delivery window is missed.
02 — The Blueprint
n8n Shipment
Tracking Pipeline

From dispatch to doorstep — every checkpoint captured, every customer informed, every delay flagged. Full visibility for operations and customers simultaneously.

n8n canvas — shipment tracking pipeline
Dispatch
📦
Waybill
Created
Shipment waybill created in WMS. n8n captures: consignee details, origin, destination, contents, declared value. Tracking number generated and sent to customer via WhatsApp.
🗺️
Route
Planned
Optimal route calculated based on: traffic data, fuel efficiency, delivery time windows, and driver availability. Assigned to driver with full route instructions.
→
Track
📍
GPS
Updates
Driver app sends GPS coordinates every 15 minutes. n8n calculates ETA based on current position vs destination. Updates customer and operations dashboard in real time.
🏭
Hub
Check-ins
Driver scans waybill QR at each hub. n8n records time, hub location, and condition check. Automatic milestone update sent to customer.
→
Detect
⚠️
Delay
Detection
n8n compares ETA vs promised delivery window every 30 mins. If ETA will miss window: operations notified immediately. Customer gets proactive delay message before window passes.
→
Notify
📱
Customer
WhatsApp
Automatic milestone messages: "Dispatched from Lagos" → "Arrived Ibadan Hub" → "Out for delivery, ETA 2pm" → "Delivered". Customer tracks without calling once.
📊
Ops
Dashboard
Live operations map: all active shipments, driver positions, ETAs, and delay flags. Operations manager sees everything from one screen. No phone calls to drivers to check status.
