01 — The Problem
The Rota That
Takes All Friday

Hospital scheduling is one of the most complex operational puzzles in any organisation — balancing skills, hours, leave, ratios, and preferences simultaneously. Done manually, it takes hours, produces errors, and creates conflicts.

⏰
3-Hour Weekly Exercise
Ward manager spends every Friday afternoon building next week's rota. Cross-checking leave requests, specialisation requirements, rest period rules, and staff preferences. Clinical management time spent on admin.
❌
Scheduling Conflicts
Two senior nurses assigned to the same night shift when both requested it off. One doctor scheduled 14 days straight without a break. Minimum ratio not met on Sunday morning. Discovered by the staff, not the manager.
📱
Last-Minute Sick Cover
Staff calls in sick. Ward manager calls around manually. Takes 45 minutes to find cover. Meanwhile a shift is understaffed. An automated on-call system would find a qualified, available replacement in 3 minutes.
📊
No Overtime Tracking
Staff accumulate hours above contract without anyone noticing until the payroll discrepancy. Fatigue builds undetected. Both a financial risk and a patient safety risk.
✅ n8n builds the weekly rota automatically — checking all constraints, sending to staff for confirmation, and managing sick cover requests with automated replacement finding.

Automated validation: no staff scheduled >48hrs/week without consent, no back-to-back night shifts, minimum ratio met every shift. Flags any violations before publication.
