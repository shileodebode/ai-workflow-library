01 — The Problem
The Enquiry That
Nobody Answered

In Nigerian real estate, the buyer who doesn't hear back within 2 hours is already talking to another agent. Most enquiries come during evenings and weekends — exactly when no one is available to respond.

🌐
Multi-Channel Chaos
Enquiries from website, Jiji, PropertyPro, WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, and referrals — tracked in 5 different places. Agents miss weekend WhatsApp messages. Monday morning brings a pile of cold leads.
⏰
Slow Response = Lost Deal
Research shows 78% of buyers go with the agent who responds first. In Lagos real estate, a 3-hour response gap can mean the difference between closing and losing a ₦50M transaction.
🎭
No Lead Qualification
Agents spend equal time on tyre-kickers asking "how much is 3 bedroom in Lekki?" as they do on serious buyers with funds ready. No qualification system means no prioritisation.
📊
No Nurture Pipeline
Lead enquires, gets a response, goes silent. Agent never follows up. Three months later the buyer purchases with a competitor who stayed in touch. A simple automated nurture sequence would have kept the relationship warm.
✅ n8n captures every enquiry from every channel, responds in 90 seconds with property details, qualifies the lead with AI questions, and routes serious buyers to agents with full context.
