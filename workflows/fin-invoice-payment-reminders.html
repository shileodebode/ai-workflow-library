01 — The Problem
Late Payments Are
Killing Cash Flow

In Nigeria and across Africa, late B2B payments are endemic. The average invoice is paid 45 days past due — not because clients can't pay, but because nobody consistently follows up.

📬
Nobody Follows Up
Finance sends an invoice, adds it to a spreadsheet, and hopes. When it's not paid, they send one awkward email a week later. Then nothing. 30% of invoices are paid late because nobody chased systematically.
😬
Relationship Fear
Finance staff avoid aggressive follow-up because they don't want to damage client relationships. Automation removes the human awkwardness — the system chases, not a person. Tone escalates on a schedule.
📊
No AR Visibility
No real-time view of what's owed, what's overdue, and by how much. Finance discovers cash flow problems at month-end — not 3 weeks earlier when something could be done.
🔄
Manual Everything
Pulling ageing reports from accounting software, writing individual reminder emails, tracking responses in a spreadsheet. Finance spends 6+ hours a week on AR management that automation handles in minutes.
✅ n8n monitors every invoice, fires a calibrated 4-touch reminder sequence, detects payment, and escalates to a human only when genuinely needed — fully automatic.
02 — The Blueprint
n8n Invoice Reminder
Pipeline

Invoice sent → n8n monitors the due date → fires reminders at the right times → detects payment → stops automatically. No human involvement unless escalation required.

n8n canvas — invoice reminder pipeline
Trigger
🧾
Invoice
Created
New invoice created in Xero/QuickBooks/Wave triggers n8n. Due date, client info, and payment terms extracted automatically.
⏰
Due Date
Monitor
n8n cron checks open invoices daily. Calculates days until/since due. Triggers reminder sequence based on schedule.
→
Remind
🧠
GPT-4o
Tone
GPT-4o calibrates tone per reminder: T-7 (warm), Due (neutral), T+3 (direct), T+7 (firm). Also adjusts for client relationship: key account = always gentler.
📧
Email
Send
Reminder sent from finance@company.com with invoice PDF attached. Personalised subject line, client name, exact amount, bank details, and payment link.
→
Detect
✅
Payment
Detect
Xero/Paystack webhook fires when payment received. n8n immediately stops reminder sequence and sends receipt confirmation to client.
→
Escalate
🚨
Human
Escalation
T+14 and no payment: Finance Director alerted via Slack. Client flagged for hold on new work. Legal notice template generated for Director review.
📊
AR
Dashboard
All invoices tracked in real-time: total outstanding, ageing buckets (0-30, 31-60, 60+ days), collection rate, DSO trend. Finance Director reviews every morning.
