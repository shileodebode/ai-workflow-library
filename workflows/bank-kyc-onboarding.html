01 — The Problem
KYC That Drives
Customers Away

For fintechs, microfinance banks, and lending platforms, clunky manual KYC is the number one reason customers abandon account opening. Every extra form, every branch visit, every manual check is a dropout point.

📋
Branch Visit Required
Customer walks to branch with documents. Teller collects, scans, submits for review. Review takes 3 days. Account opens on day 4. Competitor fintech does it in 10 minutes on the customer's phone. Which one wins?
🔄
Manual Verification Backlogs
Compliance team manually cross-referencing NIN numbers, verifying addresses, checking PEP/sanction lists. 200 applications per week × 30 minutes each = 100 hours of compliance work. All automatable.
⚠️
Compliance Risk
Manual KYC checks are inconsistent. One analyst is thorough, another rushes. PEP list checked by some, skipped by others. Manual process = variable compliance = regulatory risk.
📊
No Funnel Visibility
How many applications were started? How many abandoned at document upload? How many failed BVN check? Without data on the funnel, conversion optimisation is impossible.
✅ n8n orchestrates end-to-end digital KYC: document upload, NIN/BVN verification via NIBSS API, AML/PEP screening, liveness check, and account opening — fully automated, fully compliant.
