The Problem

Silent Guests. Viral Complaints.

Most Nigerian hotels get a fraction of the reviews they deserve — and when bad ones land, there's no system to respond, recover, or intercept the damage before it's public.

🤐
No Review Requests

Happy guests leave without a prompt. Only frustrated guests feel compelled to review — creating a skewed, negative dataset online.

⏱️
Slow Responses

Reviews sit unanswered for days or weeks. Prospective guests see no management care — and book elsewhere.

💥
Viral Complaints

A 2-star review about a billing error goes viral on Twitter before the hotel even knows about it. Zero interception.

📉
Declining Rankings

TripAdvisor and Google rank hotels by review velocity and response rate. Inaction means falling rankings — fewer bookings.

Automation Workflow
Reviews & Reputation Pipeline · Grand Continental Lagos
CAPTURE
🔗Review Webhook
Monitors Google Reviews and TripAdvisor APIs continuously. The moment a new review is posted, n8n fires instantly — even at 3am. Zero polling delay.
→
🧠Sentiment Scoring
GPT-4o scores each review: 5-star (celebrate), 4-star (acknowledge + improve), 3-star or below (escalate immediately). Scores are logged to Sheets.
RESPOND
✍️GPT-4o Draft Response
Writes a brand-appropriate response in seconds: warm gratitude for 5-stars, gracious acknowledgment for 4-stars, de-escalation language for 2-3 stars.
→
🚨Manager Alert (≤3★)
For reviews rated 3 stars or below, Slack alert fires to GM immediately: guest name, review text, draft response, and billing/incident details pulled from PMS.
INTERCEPT
📲Private Guest Outreach
For negative reviews, WhatsApp message sent privately to guest within 10 minutes: personal apology, explanation, and resolution offer — before the review spreads.
→
📤Response Posted
After manager approval (or auto-post for 5-star reviews), the GPT-4o response is posted publicly to Google or TripAdvisor. Typical response time: under 2 hours.
REPORT
📊Reputation Score Update
Google Sheets reputation tracker updated with new rating, response time, sentiment trend, and platform. Weekly email report sent to GM every Monday at 8am.
