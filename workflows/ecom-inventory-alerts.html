01 — The Problem
Selling Items You
Don't Have

For e-commerce businesses, inventory errors are revenue killers — selling items that are out of stock, missing reorder triggers, and having stock counts that differ between platforms. All fixable with automation.

😱
Overselling
3 units left in stock. Customer orders on website — 2 units. Another orders on Jumia — 2 units. Both orders confirmed. Now you have 1 unit and 2 confirmed orders. No automation = no synchronised inventory = orders you can't fulfil.
⏰
Manual Stock Checking
Owner walks to warehouse to count stock. Updates the spreadsheet. Updates the website. Updates Jumia. Updates Konga. Takes 2 hours. Done once a week. By the time it's updated, the count is already wrong again.
📦
Missed Reorder Windows
Best-selling sneaker goes to 0 units. Nobody notices until 20 customers get "out of stock" error. Supplier contacted. 2-week delivery lead time. 2 weeks of missed sales that could have been prevented with a 5-unit alert.
📊
No Sales Velocity Data
How many units of the Ankara Top sell per day? What's the reorder lead time? What's the optimal reorder point? Without velocity data, reorder decisions are gut feel — leading to either stockouts or overstock.
✅ n8n monitors stock levels in real time across all channels, alerts before stockouts, auto-generates supplier purchase orders, and pauses listings when stock hits zero — then re-activates on restocking.

n8n Inventory
Intelligence

Every sale deducts from real-time stock. Every return adds back. Alert at reorder point. Auto-reorder from supplier. Pause listings at zero. Resume on arrival. Stock accuracy maintained automatically.

Deduct
Every order deducts from inventory in real time — not at shipping. All channels (website, Jumia, Konga, WhatsApp) update the same inventory count simultaneously. No overselling possible.

Re-add
Returned and verified items automatically added back to inventory with condition status. Good condition → immediately resalable. Damaged → flagged, excluded from available count.
