01 — The Problem
Compliance That
Consumes Your Team

Nigerian financial institutions face a heavy regulatory reporting burden: CBN returns, NFIU suspicious transaction reports, PENCOM pension remittances, SEC filings. All with rigid deadlines and severe fines for non-compliance.

📅
Deadline Pressure
CBN monthly returns due by the 10th. NFIU STRs due within 24 hours of suspicious transaction detection. PENCOM remittance every month. One missed deadline = fine. Multiple missed = licence risk.
👩‍💼
Manual Report Building
Compliance officer pulls data from core banking system, formats it into regulatory templates in Excel, validates figures, gets sign-off, submits via regulatory portal. 40+ hours per month for a mid-size institution.
🚨
STR Detection Gaps
Suspicious Transaction Reports legally required within 24 hours of detection. Manual monitoring means STRs prepared reactively, often late. NFIU penalties for late filing are significant.
📊
No Compliance Dashboard
Board and management have no real-time view of regulatory obligation status. Are all returns filed? Any overdue? Any STR obligations pending? Without visibility, governance fails silently.
✅ n8n automates data extraction, report generation, STR detection and filing, and regulatory submission — with a live compliance calendar showing every obligation, every deadline, every status.
