01 — The Problem
Slow Quotes Kill
Momentum

You close the call. The prospect is ready to buy. Then your quote takes 3 days to arrive — and by then, they've gone cold or chosen someone else.

🐌
Quote Delays
Sales reps manually build quotes in spreadsheets or Word docs. Average time: 2–4 hours. Meanwhile, the prospect is losing interest or getting a faster competitor quote.
💸
Invoices Never Chased
Finance sends an invoice then waits. No automated reminders. 30% of invoices are paid late because nobody followed up. Cash flow suffers, awkward conversations ensue.
🔢
Pricing Errors
Manual quotes mean manual maths. Wrong line items, outdated pricing, missing discount approvals. Every error erodes trust and delays the deal close.
📊
Revenue Blindspot
No real-time view of outstanding quotes, sent invoices, overdue payments, or forecasted revenue. Decisions made on stale, incomplete data.
✅ n8n generates accurate quotes in 90 seconds, sends invoices automatically on deal close, and chases payment without anyone lifting a finger.

→

→
Approve
✅
Approval
Gate
Discount ≤10%: auto-approved. Discount 11–20%: manager approves via Slack one-click. Discount >20%: director approval required.
→
Deliver
📄
PDF
Generator
n8n creates a branded PDF quote with all line items, terms, validity period. Stored in Google Drive and attached to CRM deal record.
📧
Email
Send
Quote email sent to prospect — personalised cover note from rep's own Gmail account. Open tracking enabled. CRM activity logged.
→
Invoice
🧾
Invoice
Create
On quote acceptance (tracked via email click or DocuSign), n8n auto-creates invoice in Xero/QuickBooks and sends to client.
🔔
Payment
Reminders
Automated reminders at: 7 days before due, due date, 3 days overdue, 7 days overdue (+ Slack to accounts). Tone escalates with each touch.

04 — Simulation 2
Automated Payment
Reminder Sequence

Invoice sent. n8n watches the due date and fires a calibrated reminder sequence — polite first, direct later. No awkward manual chasing.

What Each Reminder Contains

  • 1
    T-7 days: "Friendly reminder — invoice due next week. Here's the link if you need to raise a PO first."
  • 2
    Due date: "Your invoice is due today. Payment details below for your convenience."
  • 3
    T+3 days: "This invoice is now 3 days overdue. Please arrange payment or let us know if there's an issue."
  • 4
    T+7 days: Escalation email from Finance Director. Slack alert to Accounts team. Late fee notice added.
💡 GPT-4o calibrates the tone of each reminder — warmer for long-standing clients, more direct for new accounts. Same rules, different voice.
