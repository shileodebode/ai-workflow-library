01 — The Problem
IT Team Drowning
in Basic Requests

Your IT team is highly skilled. Yet they spend most of their time resetting passwords, unlocking accounts, and answering questions any FAQ page could answer. That's expensive talent in the wrong place.

🔁
Repetitive L1 Tickets
"I forgot my password." "My VPN won't connect." "How do I set up 2FA?" These represent 60–70% of all IT tickets. Senior engineers answering them is the most expensive way to resolve the cheapest problems.
🐢
Slow Initial Response
Ticket lands in the queue. IT team checks the queue once an hour. Employee waits. Productivity stops. For P1 issues like system outages, that delay costs the business money every minute it persists.
🔀
Wrong Engineer Assignment
Network issues routed to the security team. Software licences sent to infrastructure. Every misroute adds a handoff delay and a frustrated employee waiting while tickets ping between inboxes.
📊
No Trend Visibility
The same VPN issue reported by 8 people in 3 days. Nobody connects the dots. IT fixes each one individually. A systematic fix would have prevented 7 of those tickets. Pattern recognition requires data.
✅ n8n + GPT-4o auto-resolves 60% of tickets with AI-guided self-service. The rest are intelligently triaged and routed to the right engineer with full diagnosis — before they open the ticket.
