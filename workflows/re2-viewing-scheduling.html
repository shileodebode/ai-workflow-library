01 — The Problem
Scheduling That
Wastes Everyone's Time

Coordinating property viewings manually — back-and-forth messages, forgotten confirmations, no-shows, and double bookings — is one of the most friction-heavy processes in real estate.

💬
The WhatsApp Ping-Pong
"When are you free?" "How about Saturday?" "I have another showing at 10, can you do 11?" "Actually 11 doesn't work..." Six messages to book one 30-minute viewing. Multiplied by 20 prospects a week.
🚫
No-Shows
Average property viewing no-show rate: 25–35%. Agent drives to the property, waits 30 minutes, drives back. One no-show costs 2 hours of agent time. Without reminders, it's entirely avoidable.
📅
Double Bookings
Agent confirms a Saturday viewing on WhatsApp. Colleague books same slot for another prospect. Both arrive. Both feel their time was wasted. Brand reputation damaged. No centralised availability system.
📊
No Viewing Pipeline
How many viewings this week? How many converted to offers? Which properties have highest viewing-to-offer ratio? Without tracking, agents can't improve conversion and management can't measure performance.
✅ n8n enables self-service viewing booking, syncs with agent calendars in real time, sends three-touch reminders, and follows up no-shows automatically within 60 minutes.
