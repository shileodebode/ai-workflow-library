01 — The Problem
Results That Take
Weeks to Arrive

Students wait weeks for results. Parents follow up by phone. Reports are paper printouts given to children who lose them. Teachers write the same generic comments for every student. All of this is automatable.

⏰
2-Week Delay
Exams end. Results compiled in Excel by class teacher. Printed on report cards. Stamped and signed by principal. Distributed to students. 2 weeks minimum. Parents calling daily asking for results.
📝
Generic Comments
"Keep up the good work." "Needs to try harder." Every student gets the same 5 comments chosen from a shortlist. Parents don't feel seen. Students don't get actionable feedback. Opportunity missed.
📄
Lost Paper Reports
Report given to 12-year-old on Friday afternoon. It never makes it home. Parents never see it. School has no record of delivery. The most important communication of the term lost between school gate and front door.
🔍
No Performance Analytics
Which subjects are consistently low across a class? Which students show declining performance? Without analytics across the student body, academic intervention decisions are reactive and subjective.
✅ n8n publishes results to every student and parent's WhatsApp the moment they're approved — with a personalised PDF report, AI-written comments, performance trends, and next-step recommendations.
