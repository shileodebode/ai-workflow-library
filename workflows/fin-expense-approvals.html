01 — The Problem
Expense Approvals
Are Broken

Expense management is one of the most paper-heavy, policy-inconsistent, and fraud-prone processes in any organisation. Manual approval chains are slow, opaque, and easily circumvented.

📨
Email Chains & Lost Claims
Staff submit expenses via email. Finance chases managers. Managers reply when they remember. Average approval time: 5–7 days. Claims get lost. Staff stop submitting. Finance loses visibility.
📋
Policy Blindspot
Does a ₦85,000 dinner require director sign-off? Is this mileage claim within policy? Manual approvers often don't check — they just click approve. Policy enforcement is inconsistent at best.
🔍
No Fraud Detection
Duplicate receipts, inflated claims, personal expenses coded as business — all invisible to manual reviewers. The average organisation loses 5–7% of expense spend to fraud or abuse annually.
📊
Zero Spend Visibility
Finance can't see departmental spend in real time. Month-end reports are 2 weeks late and built from email chains. Budget holders have no idea where they stand until it's too late.
✅ n8n automates submission, policy checks, smart routing, approval notifications, and reimbursement — with GPT-4o flagging anomalies and a live spend dashboard.

OCR+Check
GPT-4o reads receipt image: extracts amount, date, vendor, VAT. Cross-checks against submitted form data. Flags mismatches.
📋
Policy
Check
Checks claim against policy rules: daily meal limits, hotel rate caps, mileage rates, category restrictions. Auto-rejects clear violations with explanation.
→
Route
🔀
Smart
Router
Routes by amount: ≤₦50k = line manager. ₦50k–250k = department head. >₦250k = Finance Director. Overseas travel = always CFO. Routes based on claimant's org chart.
→
Approve
💬
Slack
Approve
Approver gets Slack message with full claim details, receipt thumbnail, and Approve/Reject/Query buttons. One click — no login to finance system needed.
⏰
Reminder
Escalate
No response in 24 hours: reminder sent. No response in 48 hours: claim escalated to next approver level. SLA clock always running.
→
Reimburse

Payroll
Queue
Approved claims queued for next payroll run or same-day bank transfer for amounts >₦100k. Claimant notified of expected payment date.

All approved expenses logged in real-time Google Sheets dashboard: by department, category, claimant, date. Budget utilisation visible instantly.
