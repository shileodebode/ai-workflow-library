01 — The Problem
Onboarding That
Takes Two Weeks

The period between "deal agreed" and "tenant moves in" is where leases fall through, landlords lose rent, and property managers spend weeks chasing documents that could be collected automatically in 48 hours.

📄
Document Chasing
Property manager requests ID, employment letter, pay slips, and references via WhatsApp. Tenant sends them one at a time over two weeks. Each reminder manual. Each missing document a delay to moving in.
📝
Paper Tenancy Agreements
Agreement drafted in Word, printed, signed by hand, scanned, emailed back. Takes 3–5 days minimum. If either party has corrections, the whole process starts again. All fixable with e-signatures.
💰
Rent Collection Setup
Every month the property manager manually reminds tenants to pay, records receipts, reconciles against expected rent roll. A bank mandate or automated payment system eliminates 90% of this work.
🔑
No Move-in Checklist
Tenant moves in without a documented property condition report. Six months later, damage dispute. No baseline evidence. Landlord and tenant both have different recollections of what the property looked like.
✅ n8n sends the tenant a secure onboarding portal, collects all documents with automated reminders, generates and sends the tenancy agreement for e-signature, and sets up automated rent collection — all in 48 hours.
