01 — The Problem
One Unhandled
Complaint = Real Risk

Complaints left to simmer become reviews. Reviews become reputation damage. One frustrated enterprise customer escalating to their board can cost more than a year of support budget.

👻
Invisible Frustration
Many customers don't explicitly say "complaint." They say "this is unacceptable" or "I've been waiting three weeks." Without sentiment analysis, these signals get treated as regular tickets and join the queue.
🐌
Slow Escalation Paths
Agent recognises a complaint, flags it manually, supervisor reads it tomorrow morning. Meanwhile the customer posted on LinkedIn. A 2-hour delay in a high-stakes complaint is often too late.
👑
VIP Blindspot
Enterprise clients, high-LTV customers, and media contacts get the same queue as everyone else. When their complaint sits for 8 hours, the relationship — and the contract — is at risk.
📢
Public Complaint Lag
Customer posts a 1-star review or tweets about your service. Nobody on your team sees it for hours. A fast, empathetic response in the first 30 minutes can reverse the narrative entirely.
✅ n8n + GPT-4o monitors every channel, detects complaint signals in real time, and escalates to the right person within 2 minutes — before the customer reaches for their keyboard.
